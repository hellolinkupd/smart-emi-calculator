Why Use This Calculator?

About This Loan

Home loans help individuals finance the purchase, construction or renovation of residential properties. This calculator estimates your monthly EMI, total repayment amount and total interest payable based on your selected loan amount, interest rate and tenure.

How to Use This EMI Calculator

  1. Enter the loan amount.
  2. Enter the annual interest rate.
  3. Select the loan tenure in years.
  4. The calculator automatically updates your EMI, total interest and repayment amount.
  5. Scroll down to view the amortization schedule and repayment details.
